Happy 18th Birthday

Dear Rahul,

Happy 18th Birthday! I'm so proud of everything you've accomplished and how great of a role model you have become for me. Even though you snitch on me all the time, you'll still always be the best brother I could ask for; You might even be better than Sid. Being at home will be so different without you as the oldest, and I know Papa is going to be much more emotional now. I don't know how you survived those four years; I know I'll be way better, but I'm thankful you are my older brother. You have been such a significant influence on me my whole life. You're the reason I do half the things I do. You're the reason I do MUN, I listen to the music that's always playing on my AirPods, and I learned how to make websites like this. I'm sad you're leaving home, but I know you'll do great in New York (at your low-tier Ivy). So make sure you don't forget to tell all your friends about how great your sister is and bring me back a Columbia hoodie (I have to brag about you too). Have an amazing birthday as an adult for the first time!

Your favorite sibling,

Aditi
